How To Use Trimension

Choose a primitive, adjust its proportions, then select labelled points from the side panel.

Actions appear only when the current ordered selection is valid.

Use the object list to hide, show, or delete segments, triangles, planes, angles, and labels.

For angle notation, ABC means the angle at B.
